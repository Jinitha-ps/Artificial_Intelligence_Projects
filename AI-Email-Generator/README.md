# 📧 AI Email Generator using Gemini AI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AI Email Generator** is a beginner-friendly LLM application that generates professional emails using **Google Gemini AI**. Users provide the recipient's name, email purpose, preferred tone, and additional details, and the application generates a complete email with a **subject, greeting, body, and closing**.

---

## ✨ Features

✅ Generate AI-powered emails instantly

🎯 Multiple email tones:
- 💼 Professional
- 📄 Formal
- 😊 Friendly
- 💬 Casual

🖥️ Simple and interactive Streamlit interface

📧 Generates:
- 📝 Subject
- 🙋 Greeting
- 📄 Email Body
- 🙏 Closing

✔️ Input validation for required fields

## 🚀 How It Works

1️⃣ Enter the recipient's name.

2️⃣ Enter the email purpose.

3️⃣ Select the preferred email tone.

4️⃣ Provide additional details.

5️⃣ Click **Generate Email**.

6️⃣ 🤖 Gemini AI generates a complete email.
